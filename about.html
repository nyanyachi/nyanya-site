About Nyanya Project Lab

Nyanya Project Lab is a personal mini-site for small AI-assisted tools, creative apps, and project experiments by Nyanyachi.

The goal of this site is simple: keep projects easy to find, easy to explain, and easy to maintain. Instead of combining every app into one large system, each project stays independent and is connected through this lightweight portfolio.

What I build

I mainly build practical tools around singing practice, vocal analysis, mixing support, tarot-style journaling, and project tracking. Most apps start from real personal workflows and are improved through actual use.

Project philosophy

The focus is not on making huge platforms. The focus is on small, useful tools that can be launched, tested, documented, and maintained with minimal overhead.
